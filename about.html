About the Developer
Sujit Mishra


I am Sujit Mishra, a passionate developer who enjoys building simple, useful, and impactful digital tools. This project is part of my journey to explore the power of Artificial Intelligence.

What this project does
This platform is a Text Summarizer, designed to help users quickly understand large blocks of text by converting them into short, meaningful summaries. Whether it’s articles, notes, or long paragraphs, the goal is to save time and improve productivity by extracting the most important information.

Why I built it
In today’s fast-moving digital world, information overload is a real problem. I wanted to create something that makes reading easier and faster without losing the core meaning of the content.

My vision
I aim to keep improving this tool by making it more accurate, faster, and user-friendly. In the future, I plan to add more features like multi-language support, smarter AI summarization, and document upload options.
